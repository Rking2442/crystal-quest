⚡ THE CRYSTAL QUEST OF ROYALETOWN ⚡

~ A Tale of Light & Darkness ~

The magical crystals powered RoyaleTown for generations, filling the city with warmth and light.
But pollution and overuse of resources caused strange mutations deep underground.
Small creatures known as Mini Pekkas emerged from the caves — and stole the crystals in a desperate attempt to save nature.

Now RoyaleTown sits in total darkness.
Leo, distant nephew of Greenack Treebama, takes up his lamp and pickaxe.
He must venture into the dangerous caves, defeat the Mini Pekkas,
recover all three crystals, and restore light to the town.
